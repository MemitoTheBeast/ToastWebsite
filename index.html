What is Toast?

Toast is a delicious form of bread that has been browned by heat. It’s commonly eaten with butter, jam, or other spreads.

Fun Facts About Toast

  • The first electric toaster was invented in 1893.
  • French toast dates back to ancient Rome!
  • The Maillard reaction gives toast its golden color and amazing taste.
  • The world’s largest toast mosaic was made in 2018 using over 13,000 slices!
  • Butter toast upside down to maximize flavor on your tongue!
